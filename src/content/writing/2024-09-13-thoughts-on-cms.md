# The Evolution of Content Management

Over the years, I've worked with various content management systems, from WordPress to custom-built solutions. Each has its place, but modern web development demands more flexibility.

## Why Headless CMS?

Traditional CMS platforms often come with limitations:

- **Tight coupling** between content and presentation
- **Performance overhead** from unnecessary features
- **Limited flexibility** in how content is displayed

## The Decap CMS Advantage

Decap CMS solves these issues by:

1. **Git-based workflow** - Content lives in your repository
2. **Developer-friendly** - Integrates seamlessly with modern frameworks
3. **No vendor lock-in** - Your content is just markdown files

This approach gives us the best of both worlds: a user-friendly editing interface for content creators and complete freedom for developers to build the frontend however they want.

## Looking Forward

The future of content management is clearly heading toward this decoupled approach. It's exciting to see how this will continue to evolve!
